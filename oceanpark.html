Ocean Park

Ocean Park, occupying 15 acres of beautifully landscaped gardens is located at Gandipet, which is just 15 Kilometers away from Hyderabad. It has a number of world class amusement rides, water rides and equipped with multicuisine restaurants offering best tasty south India, Chinese varieties of food at affordable prices.

Ocean Park delivers fun for all ages. For little fun seekers, they offer a whale of a time at the "Kids Pool". A safe and fun Children's play Area with a pool depth of less than two feet. Here the kids keep laughing with glee while they soak up in the sunshine having fun all the Time.
