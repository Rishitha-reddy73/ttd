Kuntala Water Falls

TThe Kuntala Waterfalls is located 12 kms from the Neredikonda villege, about 64 km from Adilabad. At Kuntala,the Kadem river cascades 45 meters down and rushes deep into the Jungles. Kuntala, the highest waterfalls in the State, offers a spectacular sight particularly when the river is flush with copious inflows. The winter months are ideal for witnessing the waterfalls, as one can also see the river in all its glory. There is an image of Lord Shiva near the water falls known as Someshwara Swami. Many devotees congregate here for Maha Shiva Ratri festivities.
